Ingredients

For the Focaccia

  • 4 cups(500gr) all purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cups(350gr) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin oil, plus more for the pan

For the Topping

  • 3 medium yellow onions sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoon olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes
  • 1/2 cups fresh
